Privacy Policy for FocusVibe

Effective Date: April 19, 2026

FocusVibe (“the app”) is a macOS status bar application designed to provide ambient sounds for productivity and focus. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how information is handled.

1. Data Collection

FocusVibe does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

No account is required
No analytics or tracking tools are used
No personal information is requested or stored
2. Audio Functionality

FocusVibe plays ambient sounds locally on your device.
All audio playback is handled entirely on-device and does not require any data transmission.

3. Permissions

FocusVibe does not request access to:

Location services
Camera or microphone
Contacts or personal files
4. Third-Party Services

FocusVibe does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

5. Data Security

Since no user data is collected or stored, no personal data is processed or transmitted.

6. Children’s Privacy

FocusVibe does not collect any personal information from users, including children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be reflected with a revised “Effective Date.”
